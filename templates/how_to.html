BULK E-MAIL SENDER

HOW TO...

○ GMAIL ACCOUNT

▻ make sure that your gmail account's 2-Step Verification is OFF (turn off)
▻ make sure that your gmail account's Less secure apps & your Google Account is OFF (turn off)
▻ make sure you have entered valid email address and password of gmail from which you want to send messages
▻ you may verify if all emails were send at your gmail account - they will appear in SENT folder
▻ according to Google there is a limit of emails that could be sent from an account - 500 emails
▻ you may also increase the limits (Google limits)
AUTHOR DO NOT RECOMMEND TO TOGGLE OFF ANY OF MENTIONED SETTINGS (2-Step Verification / Less secure apps & your Google Account) ON PRIVATE ACCOUNTS SINCE IT RESULTS IN REDUCED SECURITY OF YOUR ACCOUNT

○ EXCEL TABLE

▻ make sure that its excel file (extension: .xlsx )
▻ file contains two columns with headers: email and message as shown below
(it may also contain other columns, but those two must have exactly mentioned names)
▻ make sure that your emails are valid and there are no empty cells in email column
(after upload you will get a hint which of emails may be invalide but you still should validate them yourself ! Author do not take responsibility for undelivered messages!)
▻ emails should be delivered to any address that is valid (exist)
